# Editorial AI Assistant - Sample Edits

## Grammar Correction
**Input:** Its important to note that the teams performance has been effected by recent changes.  
**Output:** It's important to note that the team's performance has been affected by recent changes.

## Active Voice Conversion
**Input:** The decision was made by the committee.  
**Output:** The committee made the decision.

## Concision
**Input:** At this point in time, we need to make a decision about the project's future.  
**Output:** We need to decide about the project's future.

## Jargon Removal
**Input:** We need to leverage our core competencies to synergize our value proposition.  
**Output:** We should focus on our strengths to improve our customer offering.
